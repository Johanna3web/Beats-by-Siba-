## Project Overview

Beats by Siba – Hair Edition is a premium, luxury e-commerce website built to showcase and sell high-quality human hair products including wigs, bundles, closures, and frontals.  
The website emphasizes elegance, trust, and confidence, targeting customers who value softness, longevity, and flawless natural finishes.

---

## Features

- Modern, responsive, mobile-friendly design with luxury aesthetic  
- Homepage with hero section, brand messaging, and call-to-action  
- Shop pages with product categories and detailed product listings  
- Fully functional e-commerce checkout with Stripe and PayPal integration  
- Order tracking system allowing customers to track their shipments  
- Hair Care Guide and FAQs to educate customers on hair maintenance  
- About Us page sharing the brand story and values  
- Contact page with phone, email, and contact form  
- Location-based tax logic applying tax only for international orders  
- Clear Shipping & Returns policy
